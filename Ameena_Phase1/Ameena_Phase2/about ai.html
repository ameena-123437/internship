ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

what is artificial intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that enables machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence such as learning, reasoning, and problem-solving.

types of artificial intelligence

There are different types of AI including Narrow AI, General AI, and Super AI. Narrow AI is designed for specific tasks, while General AI aims to perform any intellectual task like a human.

benefits of artificial intelligence

AI helps in automation, improves efficiency, reduces human error, and provides faster decision-making in various fields like healthcare, education, and business.

why is artificial intelligence important

Artificial Intelligence is important because it helps solve complex problems, improves productivity, and supports innovation across industries.

how to learn artificial intelligence

You can learn AI by studying programming languages like Python, understanding mathematics, and practicing machine learning projects.
